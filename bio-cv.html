Biography

Mumbere Tassi Dieumerci is a multifaceted professional based in Kenya, driven by the belief that technology and spirituality can both serve as tools for transformation.

His educational journey began in Goma, Democratic Republic of Congo, where he completed his primary education at EP KATOYI. He then pursued his secondary education at ITIG / DON BOSCO, specializing in Electrical studies.

Determined to expand his knowledge, Dieumerci continued his academic path in Kenya at JKUAT in Nairobi, where he studied Information Technology.

Alongside his professional journey, Dieumerci has a deep passion for music. He began in Goma with the Uhai Choir. During this time, he was given the name “Josias”, which remains his name within the church community.

As an Electrical and IT Engineer, Dieumerci specializes in the intersection of physical infrastructure and digital security, from industrial systems to network hardening.

Beyond the lab, he is a dedicated Preacher in RPMC and Musician, aiming to inspire hope and build systems of faith.
